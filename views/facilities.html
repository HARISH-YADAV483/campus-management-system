Room allocations

For the academic session 2025‚Äì26, this hostel has been primarily allotted to first-year (fresher) students of the institute, helping them begin their college life in a safe, organized, and supportive residential environment. The hostel mainly accommodates students from the Information Technology (IT), Computer Science and Engineering (CSE), Production Engineering, Artificial Intelligence and Data Science (AIDS), and Artificial Intelligence and Machine Learning (AIML) branches. Each room is designed to accommodate three students, ensuring comfortable living while encouraging teamwork and peer bonding. In total, the hostel has 90 rooms and can accommodate more than 250 students, making it one of the key residential facilities for freshmen at NIT Kurukshetra.
